Introduction

This website serves as a portfolio for the work I realised during my fifth year at INSA Toulouse. It not only lists all the different projects I took part in, but also serves as a way to reflect on the knowledge and skills I have acquired. I followed the Innovative Smart Systems (ISS) option, which aims to mix students of three different scientific backgrounds to tackle problems from different angles, these backgrounds being either Computer Science and Networks, Automatics and Electronics, or Physics. I came through the Computer Science route, and made sure to learn as much as possible from my comrads.
This portfolio is organised by course modules, with each over-arching course modules comprised of multiple courses. The different course modules can be accessed through the top left dropdown menu for ease of navigation. I'll be going over each course in three parts: a descriptive part to relate the type of work I had to do, a technical part describing the problems that work posed and the solutions I found, and finaly an analytical part to reflect on what I took away from this experience.
On this page, you'll find a brief presentation of who I am, my Curriculum Vitae and a table summarizing the content of all the course units. Let's get to it!

Who am I?

My name is Louis Chauvet-Smart. I was born in 1998 and have French-British citizenship.

  • I joined INSA Toulouse in first year, right after getting a scientific Baccalauréat. After a general scientific first year, studying mathematics and computer science but also chemistry, physics and electronics, I chose to join the Mathematics and Computer Science second year. From there on, I decided to delve deeper into computer science, going for a Computer Science and Networks third and fourth year, finally choosing the ISS PTP for my final year.

  • During my third year, I was president of INSA Toulouse's theatre group, giving me a creative outlet and a unique way of improving my management skills.

  • During My fourth Year, I spent a semester studying at the Pohang Institute of Science and Technology (POSTECH) in Pohang, South Korea, getting to work with incredibly talented people from all over the world.

  • For my fourth year internship, I developped an online training platform for the project management firm Strategic Project Management International (SPMI), available at this link.
